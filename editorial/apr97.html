Edku: April 1997

Move backwards to March 1997.

1.
Heaven's Gate moves on.
Next week pilot disappears.
Comet? We'll bomb it.

--Ken Kaufman, kaufman@aic.gmu.edu

2.
Weather starts to warm.
Forecast bitter cold for Cubs.
Out of race by May.

--Ken Kaufman, kaufman@aic.gmu.edu

3.
Thundering footfalls
To the family kitchen
Clinton sneaks a snack

--JMHACLJ@aol.com

4.
One A-10 Warthog
A range of snow-capped mountains
One more pilot down

--Larry Cruikshank, laredog@primenet.com

5.
Texas Republic
Leaning toward destruction
We the people, lose

--kj slavan, kslavan@ipa.net

6.
Fly, Comet Hale-Bopp!
Hey, did you hear our applause?
We'll see you next time.

--C.E. (Chuck) Roberts, croberts@alumni.umbc.edu
