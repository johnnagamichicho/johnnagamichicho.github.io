Edku: March 2001

Move backwards to February 2001.

1.
Electric window,
Butt lackadaisic'ly tossed:
SUV Woman.

--David L. Ross, rossesse@famvid.com

2.
Minorities rule
On political pages:
Choking social dust.

--Marilyn Ellsworth, SFMaxwell@ij.net

3.
Not racial issues
Rather political ploys:
Crying "Wolf!" again

--Marilyn Ellsworth, SFMaxwell@ij.net

4.
All cheating leaders
Must swallow their "just desserts"
Fat cats feed big prey

--Marilyn Ellsworth, SFMaxwell@ij.net

5.
Chanting malcontents
Democratic processes
Industries flourish

--Marilyn Ellsworth, SFMaxwell@ij.net

6.
Squeaking wheels get greased.
Oft-greased wheels do get repaired.
Oft-repaired: replaced!

--Marilyn Ellsworth, SFMaxwell@ij.net

7.
Lonesome whistles blow--
Industry trends reversing
New gold mines open

--Marilyn Ellsworth, SFMaxwell@ij.net

8.
Paradigm shifting
Progressively multiplies
Industrial death

--Marilyn Ellsworth, SFMaxwell@ij.net

9.
Did those soiléd hooves
Walk on England's mountain green?
Bring my spear of fire!

--John Cho, jync@mit.edu

10.
fluorescent weevils,
with sterile cotton-dreams of
wild sexual mates.

--Pat Cummings, drpat@aht.com

11.
Cold, wet snowflakes fall,
Building on the frozen ground,
While I wait to sled.

--Seth Benson, slick08sab@yahoo.com

12.
Napster supplies files;
Free music for all to hear,
But not for too long.

--fyvretronik@hotmail.com

13.
The oil flow slows down
A clog in the line somewhere
Cheney grabs his chest

--John Cho, jync@mit.edu

14.
Kyoto Protocol
Not for George of the White House
Japan made Dad puke

--j. david johnson, pundamkuu@excite.com

15.
A campaign pledge falls
Abruptly as the NASDAQ
CO2 rising

--John Cho, jync@mit.edu

16.
Carbon dioxide
Campaign promise up the drain
Bush passing on gas

--Lee Malone, leemalone@aol.com

17.
Flies Enola Gay
Nagasaki blown away
Hanford has its day

--David I. Brager

18.
Fifty years of doubt
Atomic waste seeping out
Seeds of death about

--David I. Brager

19.
Timeless as Stonehenge
Radioactives avenge.
Japanese Revenge!

--David I. Brager

20.
Sleeping bear wakes up
Euro bull chased by mad cow
Wall Street lemmings jump

--John Cho, jync@mit.edu
