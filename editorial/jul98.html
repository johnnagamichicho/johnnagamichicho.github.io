1.

2.

3.
Darkness falls, once more,
Moon and stars again, unite,
Bliss, conquers all, night.

--Daria Kornienko, dkornienko@hotmail.com

4.
Shadows glaze through waves,
The haze, unforgettable,
Thrilling current, sea.

--Daria Kornienko, dkornienko@hotmail.com

5.
Smell-less but most quaint,
Embroiders of carpet earth,
Gem the velvet sod.

--Daria Kornienko, dkornienko@hotmail.com
