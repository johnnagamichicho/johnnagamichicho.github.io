Edku: August 2001

Move backwards to July 2001.

1.
Wonks mingle with moose
Anarchists peer from bear suits
It's Kananaskis

--John Cho

2.
Fetid summer wind
Smothers a whole continent
Limbaugh exhaling

--Peter Hartikka

3.
Oil from the tundra
Blacker than Dick Cheney's heart
Feeds our SUVs

--Peter Hartikka

4.
Naked, hungry, cold
But at least I have my guns:
Libertarian

--Peter Hartikka

5.
Tommy Suharto
He sure plays a mean pinball
No replay for judge

--John Cho

6.
Pied piper shaman
An ectoplasmic Elvis
In day-glo suede shoes

--Bliles31@aol.com

7.
Bush on vacation
John Cho on vacation too
A conspiracy?

--Lee Malone

8.
Alaskan wildlife
Me, you, and the caribou
George Bush is on top

--Jennifer Gardner

9.
I love my kids, but...
Sleeping peaceful in their beds,
The best time of day...

--Linda Franklin

10.
Dark and gruesome Anne,
Says "Lets play the Weakest Link"
Then savages all.

--Linda Franklin

11.
A question got wrong,
So "You are the weakest link,
Goodbye!" says Host Anne.

--Linda Franklin

12.
Summer's long and hot.
School's out, the children are home.
HURRY UP AUTUMN!!

--Linda Franklin

13.
A man in love weeps
Afraid of commitment runs
Abusive men--creeps

--Aurora Munoz
