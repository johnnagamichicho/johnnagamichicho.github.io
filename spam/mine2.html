SPAM Haiku by the SHAM (201- )

201.
What do Oscar Wilde
And SPAM loaves have in common?
Homo genius.

202.
SPAM volcano blows.
Stratosphere laden with pork.
Gorgeous pink sunsets.

203.
National campaign:
Dare to keep children off SPAM.
Hormel bombs White House.

204.
SPAM in Vienna
Declares in a stirring speech,
"Ich bin ein Wiener."

205.
Spamlet: Danish ham.
Lite version: Hormel Gibson.
Cooked up by Bacon?

206.
Saddam's big surprise:
Opening the crate marked "FOOD"
He finds SPAM inside.
