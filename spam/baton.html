The SHAM is Retiring!

Having ruled over the SPAM-ku archive since its inception on June 14, 1995, the SPAM Haiku Archive Master (SHAM) is ready to pass on the torch to a worthy successor. In effect, he will become a SHAM emeritus (SHAME). Anyone who is SERIOUSLY interested in this UNPAID position, contact the SHAM (). Please explain why you are crazy enough to take on such a task. Applicants must have about 10 MB of space on a Web server. Access to cgi-bin programming makes the archiving task much simpler, since an automated submission script already exists.
