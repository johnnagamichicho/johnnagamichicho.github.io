Tropopause structure and its "weakening" as seen by Indian MST radar

This paper aims at the detection of finer structure of tropopause including "weakening" by Indian MST radar aided with temperature profile. Four campaigns have been carried out using the Indian MST radar at Gadanki near Tirupati (13.5 deg N, 79.2 deg E) during January 3-8, November 9-16 and December 5-10, 1994, June 7-24, 1995. The results show occurrence of the tropopause "weakening" for several times during January 3-8, November 9-16 and December 5-10, 1994. But the results during 3-8 January, 1994 are presented here. In addition, a plausible mechanism which can qualitatively account for the cause of tropopause "weakening" has been addressed here.
